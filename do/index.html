what. we. do.

our portfolio includes a variety of services and tools by which our clients can explore and understand the learning experiences we co-design. some of our projects are broad enough in scope to integrate many of these services detailed below.

educational + psychological research

winded.vertigo offers educational and psychological research services through their collective members and their extensive networks. they strategically engage specific expertise based on project requirements. their methodology focuses on designing targeted studies around clear learning questions, ensuring interventions yield measurable and actionable outcomes.

developmental psychology consultation

developmental psychology consultation service offered by a collective of experts who specialize in designing developmentally appropriate learning experiences. they focus on creating inclusive educational environments that balance challenge with skill level, helping learners achieve flow states without experiencing boredom or overwhelm.

communication assets

the team focuses on sharing processes and evidence-based insights through PDF briefs and workshop materials. the team views their outputs as opportunities for artistic expression while distilling complex ideas for communications teams, creating accessible assets from complex concepts.

psychometrics

measurement supports educators and learners by enabling risk-taking and curiosity during playful learning. this approach utilizes advanced modeling techniques that harness modern computational power to apply machine learning for rapid evidence processing. AI-enabled tools integrate measurement directly into learning experiences, operating behind the scenes as supportive elements.

learning experience design

a team of teachers, educational psychologists, and developmentalists create tailored learning experiences that consider learners' contextual differences, developmental needs, and collaborative learning opportunities. their approach emphasizes intrinsic motivation over traditional gamification or edutainment methods.

museum exhibit design + evaluation

researching and enhancing learning experiences that occur outside traditional school environments. through mixed methods research, this service supports museums, libraries, and community centers in designing engaging exhibits that foster playful learning.

skills assessment + monitoring

the collective combines theoretical expertise with practical skills to create comprehensive assessment systems powered by machine learning and AI. their holistic framework enables quick, detailed feedback that serves dual purposes: supporting individual skill development while evaluating the effectiveness of learning products and interventions.

toy + game design

experienced advisors offering scientific expertise in children's play and toy development. the team specializes in designing experimental studies and focus groups to evaluate toy functions and attributes through A/B testing, ensuring designs contribute meaningfully to children's growth across developmental domains.

educational programme evaluation

educational programme evaluation employs a monitoring, evaluation, and learning (MEL) approach to transform organizations into learning entities. the methodology prioritizes creating evidence and reporting that effectively addresses stakeholder communication needs.

what. we. do.

our portfolio includes a variety of services and tools by which our clients can explore and understand the learning experiences we co-design. some of our projects are broad enough in scope to integrate many of these services detailed below.

educational + psychological research

winded.vertigo not only includes members from its collective, but also the broad networks each of them brings along. a collective allows us to call on some members when their skills, experience, and network are appropriate. We design studies focused on specific learning questions, so that the impact of interventions is clear and actionable.

developmental psychology consultation

our collective includes developmental psychologists who focus on the design of learning experiences that are within appropriate developmental windows. we see these as opportunities for learning to be inclusive, but also support the flow experience that learners can attain when the challenge pushes their skills with neither boredom nor overwhelm.

communication assets

our team believes that sharing our process and evidence-based insights are opportunities to distill complex ideas into assets for comms teams. we think of outputs as opportunities for artistic expression.

psychometrics

measurement can be our friend in supporting educators and learners to take risks and explore their curiosity during playful learning. we use cutting edge modeling techniques that leverage modern computational capacities to apply machine learning to rapidly process evidence and AI-enabled tools that allow measurement to be integrated into the learning experiences themselves albeit behind the scenes as support rather than as directors.

learning experience design

our team includes teachers, educational psychologists, and developmentalists to design learning experiences tailored to learners’ needs. we account for their contextual differences, developmental needs, while engaging in how they can share learning together. we think of the motivation to learn as stronger than those pitched by traditional gamification or edutainment approaches that leverage blunt applications of extrinsic motivators and neurochemical reactions (see dopamine).

museum exhibit design + evaluation

learning occurs outside of school walls, and community centers like libraries and children’s & science museums. we conduct mixed methods research to help: museums to design for playful learning; children to engage and return to learning exhibits; and caregivers to better understand how these experiences help their children develop and thrive.

skills assessment + monitoring

*priorities for educational outcomes have changed. these changes impact learning experiences, professional development (badges & credentials) and even gaming. we hope that the end is near for dopamine-hunting and click-bait entertainment in lieu of the human motivations from learning and development. our team is eminently qualified in both theory and skills to provide high-quality skills assessment and monitoring. by using machine learning and AI, we take this work even further into its potential to support development through feedback that is both detailed and quick. informed by a holistic skills framework, we build skills monitoring systems that provide insights on both the impact on the person, but also feedback on the design capabilities of your products or interventions.

toy + game design

our team comes with years of experience advising on developmental implications of children’s play with toys. this play included designing experimental studies or focus groups to A:B test functions and attributes. We are also adept in scientifically rigorous methods for gathering evidence for impact on children’s holistic skills development.

educational programme evaluation

our approach to program evaluation uses monitoring, evaluation, and learning (MEL) approach to support organizations or programmes to become learning organizations. we are keen on providing evidence and reporting that meets the messaging needs of stakeholders, such that policy-makers may prefer both narratives and quantitative methods for reporting impacts on behaviour.
